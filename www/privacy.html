Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how InFlight ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our website (the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

  • Public Flight & Pilot Data: The Service functions by fetching publicly available data from flight simulation networks. This includes your User ID, Username, Callsign, flight plan, live position, and pilot statistics. This information is, by its nature, public within the simulation community.
  • Usage Data: We may automatically collect information about how you access and use the Service, such as your IP address, browser type, device information, and pages visited. This is used for analytics and to improve service stability.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies or similar technologies to store your preferences (like map settings) and for analytics. You can instruct your browser to refuse cookies, but some parts of the Service may not function properly.
  • Payment Information: If you choose to purchase a "Pro" subscription (when available), our third-party payment processor (e.g., Stripe, PayPal) will collect your payment information. We do not store your full credit card details on our servers. We only receive confirmation of your payment and subscription status.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide and operate the Service (e.g., display your flight on the map).
  • To personalize your experience (e.g., display your pilot report).
  • To process transactions and manage your "Pro" subscription (when available).
  • To monitor and analyze usage and trends to improve the Service.
  • To ensure the security and stability of the Service.

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

Your live flight data (callsign, username, position) is publicly visible on our Service by default, as this is the core function of a live flight tracker.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data with:

  • Third-Party Service Providers: Such as Mapbox (for maps), OpenWeatherMap (for weather data), and our payment processor (for handling subscriptions). These providers have their own privacy policies governing their use of data.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. When you enter payment information, that data is encrypted and transmitted directly to our third-party payment processor.

5. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have, we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date. Your continued use of the Service after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new policy.
